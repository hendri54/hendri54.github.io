    Schedule of Classes¶

    No class on Oct-12 (University Day), Oct-21 (fall break), Nov-25 (Thanksgiving).

    Short Run¶

    • Aug-19 (Thu): Introduction, IS-LM model
    • Aug-24 (Tue): IS/LM Equilibrium, PP
    • Aug-26 (Thu): The term paper, Monetary Policy
    • Aug-31 (Tue): Fiscal Deficits, PP

    Medium Run¶

    • Sep-2 (Thu): Labor market, PP
    • Sep-7 (Tue): Labor market (continued)
    • Sep-9 (Thu): AS-AD model, PP (covers Phillips Curve)
    • Sep-14 (Tue): AS-AD model (continued)
    • Sep-16 (Thu): AS-AD model (continued)
    • Sep-21 (Tue): Inflation and unemployment
    • Sep-23 (Thu): Inflation expectations and monetary policy, PP

    Open Economy¶

    • Sep-28 (Tue): Trade deficits, PP
    • Sep-30 (Thu): Trade deficits (continued)
    • Oct-7 (Thu): IS-LM model, PP
    • Oct-14 (Thu): IS-LM floating exchange rate
    • Oct-19 (Tue): IS-LM fixed exchange rate
    • Oct-26 (Tue): AS-AD model, Policy analysis, PP
    • Oct-28 (Thu): Costs and benefits of international trade

    Expectations¶

    • Nov-2 (Tue): Asset prices, PP
    • Nov-4 (Thu): Model summary

    Inequality¶

    • Nov-9 (Tue): Inequality facts, Earnings inequality
    • Nov-11 (Thu): The top 1 percent, Intergenerational mobility

    Long-run Growth¶

    • Nov-16 (Tue): Growth and ideas
    • Nov-18 (Thu): Romer model, PP
    • Nov-23 (Tue): Policy implications
    • Nov-30 (Tue): Last class. All questions answered! (or almost all...).
